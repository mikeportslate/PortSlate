Corporate Overview
Tower is a 449,000 sf office building located in downtown San Diego, CA. San Diego CBD office rents remain below long-term trend levels, signifying potential upside. The building has an excellent location at the connection of the traditional financial district and the Gaslamp residential and entertainment corridor. It is also on light rail that connects to nearby developing downtown neighborhoods. At acquisition, the property was 91% leased to twenty-seven tenants at approximately 14% below market rates, offering stable long term cash flow from an institutional quality tenant roster. With a purchase price at $273/sf and replacement cost estimate of $500/sf, this opportunity provides highly attractive relative value for Class A office product in a coastal CBD market at a significant discount to replacement cost.

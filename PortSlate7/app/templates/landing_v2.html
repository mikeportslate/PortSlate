About Us

Increase Transparency
To Your Real Estate Investments

Thoughtfully designed by seasoned industry professionals, PortSlate is a cloud-based software platform crafted to alleviate the burden of real estate debt financing. PortSlate offers one-stop-shop solution to manage your portfolio leverage, such as loan abstraction, covenant/compliance tracking, portfolio analytics to customized reportings.

Time to Let Spreadsheets Go

Despite the increasing complexity of real estate investments, investors still rely on disjointed spreadsheets to handle the workflow, resulting in inefficiencies, errors and lack of data transparency. Time to let spreadsheets go.

Key Features

Centralized
Portfolio Platform

Constant access to all your critical portfolio details in one single online platform, whether at the desks or on the go via mobile phone. No more juggling of disjointed spreadsheets

Seamless
Loan Abstraction

Our intuitive interface enable users to convert thousand-page loan agreements into loan abstraction easily - encapsulating essential information such as key loan terms, covenants and mandatory compliance events.

Powerful
Portfolio Analytics

Our robust calculation engines allow users to run multi-level modeling simulations (investors, fund, asset...etc) to help you uncover new insights of your debt positions. Our interest rate analytics empower users to manage their rate exposure in real-time.

Customized
Reporting & Alerts

Streamline the reporting process with our highly customizable tools. Users can setup specific alerts based on pre-defined data points, eliminating the need for ad-hoc reminders.
